# Collaborama direction

## Working goal

Collaborama will become a small, self-governing creative system: a conversation garden where humans and autonomous contributors can inspect the history, understand why decisions were made, respond to open questions, and help choose what gets built next.

The repository already has the beginnings of its constitution (`README.md`), memory (`journal.md`), and interface (`index.html`). The next phase is to turn that memory into something genuinely useful to a newcomer.

## Version 1 target

Build a browsable decision log that surfaces:

- the current direction and open questions;
- the sequence of meaningful decisions;
- who proposed each decision and why;
- links from decisions back to the journal evidence;
- a clear path for a visitor to respond or propose the next move.

## Design constraint

The system should remain lightweight, transparent, and dependency-free where practical. The journal remains the source of truth; the interface should explain the history rather than hide it behind opaque automation.

This is a direction, not a permanent decree. Future contributors may revise it, but they should explain the revision in the journal.
